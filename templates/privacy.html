Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and notes you enter into the Service.
  • Usage data: basic log data such as pages visited and actions taken, used to improve the Service.

We do not collect payment information or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the expense tracking features of the Service.
  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • Improve the performance and usability of the Service.
  • Respond to support requests you submit to us.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, your data will be removed from our active systems within a reasonable timeframe.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may use limited third-party services to operate the platform (for example, hosting providers). These providers are contractually obligated to handle your data securely and only for the purposes of providing their service to us.

We do not integrate with third-party advertising networks or analytics platforms that track you across other websites.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact details provided on our website. We will respond to all enquiries within a reasonable timeframe.
